Staying Safe on Social Media



URL
 Two Factor Authentication
Site Author
 Linda Rosencrance
Abstract
 This site talks about what two factor authenticators are as well as the general types of authentication to help social media privacy and security. It lists the general steps of enabling 2FA and the benefits of it. This site also talks about some physical security products that exist too. With how easy it can be to contact someone on social media to cause them harm, this is a very insightful article on how to keep safe on social media and ways to prevent falling into the trap of someone dangerous.  



URL
 Scammers and How to Avoid Them
Site Author
 Federal Trade Commission
Abstract
 The Federal Trade Commission for Consumer Advice website discusses the importance of looking out for scammers by mentioning their go-to tactics and how to avoid falling for one. This mentions online ads, and since a good portion of people are on social media we are more prone to seeing these scammer ads all the time. They make themselves very believable and falling for a scam online can lead a scammer to do much worse. Phone calls and pop-up warnings are common ways people can fall into a trap and this site goes into depth as to how to get yourself out of a scamming situations and how to prevent it from happening again.
