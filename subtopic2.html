 Augmented Reality
Site Author
 Lilach Bullock
Abstract
 This site brought by Forbes talks about a form of technology many people probably do not know the name of. Augmented reality is heavily embedded with social media and Bullock goes into detail about what it is and where it can be found. On platforms like Snapchat and Instagram, augmented reality comes into play in terms of the filters consumers use daily. It is a new way to place yourself in a digital world inspired by real life. The site also covers how far AR technology can go and how we think it can impact our future as it becomes more accessible everyday.

 Artificial Intelligence
Site Author
 Forbes Technology Council
Abstract
 This site on Forbes discusses Artificial Intelligence in Social Media. It covers different topics such as, AI in marketing, when brands want to find influencers to work with, AI can help them see who would get the most sales. This site also talks about algorithms and how artificial intelligence is used to curate the feeds and for you pages, of every social media consumer in the world. Making it completely personalized and unique to the user, AI learns more about users the more time they spend on these apps. As we learn about how AI has shaped social media, the site also touches on the challenges and limitations of AI too.
