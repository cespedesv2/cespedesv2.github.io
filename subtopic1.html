The Evolution of Social Media

URL
How Social Media Evolved
Site Author
 MaryVille University


Abstract
 This site talks about the evolution of social media dating back to 1844 (the beginning years of social media) to present time as it goes into depth of the technological advances that have occurred and how this now affects the day to day lives of every person. Showing various kinds of statistics from different social media platforms, we really learn how new technologies in social media have shaped different businesses and consumers over time.



URL
 The Influence of Technology on Social Media
Site Author
 Stefan Thurairatnam
Abstract
 This site also touches on the growth of social media. It dives into what it is and what it does. It explains the way technology has impacted its development and how now people can make social media their profession. This website mentions the importance of this growth in technology and how interconnected social media and technology are. The site author also gives a brief description on the main sources of technology that many platforms use today such as, Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality, etc.
